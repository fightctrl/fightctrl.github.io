USING DESIGN TO CONVEY THOUGHTS AND FEELINGS THAT CAN'T BE EASILY EXPLAINED

Hey, I'm Melanie. I'm a multi-disciplinary designer based in Perth/Boorloo, Western Australia. I love playing around with colours, type and visual styles, but most importantly, I aim to harness the power of design to create stories and experiences that need to be shared.
